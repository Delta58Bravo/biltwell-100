⛽ Fuel
Truck Fuel
Fill up in Victorville or Barstow on the way up. Ridgecrest has multiple gas stations (Chevron, Arco, etc.) — top off when you arrive. Fill again before driving home.
FE501s Race Fuel
The FE501s has a ~2.2 gal tank. At desert pace (~35 mpg) you're looking at ~75–80 miles range. The race is 100 miles (4 laps × ~25 miles). You will need pit fuel. Plan your pit location and have extra fuel at pit. Bring a 1-gallon race jug minimum.
🏕️ Camping
Options
The Football Field OHV area is BLM land — dispersed camping is generally allowed. Many racers camp right at the venue. Bring everything you need — no hookups. Alternatively, Ridgecrest has hotels and a Walmart for supplies.
📋 Race Rules Quick Ref
Start Format
Rubber band Grand Prix start by class. Fastest classes go first. Know when your wave goes — Dual Sport Intermediate will be mid-pack in the modern bike order.
Numbers
Given randomly at registration. Sticky numbers provided — apply OVER your existing numbers. #271.
Course Closes
4:00 PM hard cutoff. All riders off trail by 5:00 PM. Do not be caught out after 4.
Scoring
Helmet transponder (primary) + fender card (backup). Both must be present and correct.
No Team Riding
One rider per bike, per entry. No exceptions.
